Bullseye Kettle Corn is a great way to raise money for any organization! We have experience with fundraising events for schools, sports communities, and many other non-profit organizations. Bullseye can help you reach your goals through at-event sales or through direct sales.

If you are looking to raise money through direct sales (i.e. Girl Scout cookie and candy bar fundraising methods), we offer a variety of gourmet flavors and our traditional Bullseye Kettle Corn. The kettle corn can be packaged in individual bags and/or popcorn tins with up to 3 flavors.

Our hot and fresh kettle corn is sure to bring smiles to your benefactors and bring them back year after year!
Contact us to see how Bullseye Kettle Corn can meet your fundraising needs.
